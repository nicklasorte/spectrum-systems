SPECTRUM COORDINATION SESSION — 5.9 GHz V2X Band
Date: 2026-01-15
Attendees: Harriet Stone (Chair), Wei Zhang (Industry), Piotr Kaminski (Regulator)

--- TRANSCRIPT BEGIN ---

Harriet Stone: Let's start with the coexistence framework outcome. We have agreed to adopt a dynamic channel access protocol for the 5.9 GHz V2X band.

Wei Zhang: Confirmed. Dynamic channel access protocol — that's the right call for V2X coexistence.

Piotr Kaminski: Agreed. Dynamic protocol adopted.

Harriet Stone: On the interference floor: we are setting it at -103 dBm/MHz for V2X operations.

Wei Zhang: -103 dBm/MHz interference threshold for V2X. Confirmed.

Piotr Kaminski: -103 dBm/MHz is the accepted standard.

Harriet Stone: Now, the reference minutes that were circulated before this meeting stated the threshold was -100 dBm/MHz and the channel access was TDMA-fixed.

Wei Zhang: That document was wrong. We changed both of those in the last session.

Piotr Kaminski: The pre-meeting minutes are incorrect. Today's decisions supersede them.

Harriet Stone: Correct. For the record: dynamic channel access at -103 dBm/MHz. The pre-circulated minutes were based on a superseded draft.
