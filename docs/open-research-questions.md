# Open Research Questions

- What schemas are required for spectrum study outputs across diverse services and scenarios?
- How should simulation results be normalized across studies to enable comparison and meta-analysis?
- How can institutional spectrum knowledge be structured for retrieval and grounding?
- How should AI-generated engineering text be validated for technical accuracy and policy compliance?
- What confidence and calibration methods best signal when human review is mandatory?
- How can provenance be preserved end-to-end without hindering analyst velocity?
